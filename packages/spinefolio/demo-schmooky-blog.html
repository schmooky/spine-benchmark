Spinefolio Canvas Seamlessly Inside Blog Prose

This demo mirrors the exact visual language from schmooky.dev and embeds the canvas like a native article block.

The widget below uses the same dark surfaces, muted typography, and accent orange from your portfolio. Controls are re-skinned to blend in with article UI while keeping interaction intact.

Drop-in Blog Usage

Use class spinefolio-theme-schmooky on the widget container and include ./themes/schmooky.css next to spinefolio.css.

Tip: In your post templates, wrap the widget with .spinefolio-embed-schmooky to get spacing/caption rhythm identical to your prose sections.
